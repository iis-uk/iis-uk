International Isotope Society UK Group 25th Annual Symposium

A one day event covering the synthesis and applications of isotopically labelled compounds to be held on Friday, 11th November 2016 at The Møller Centre in Cambridge, UK.

Download Registration Form

This year we celebrate our 25th annual symposium and a highlight of the meeting will be the Melvin-Calvin award winner Professor William Kerr from the University of Strathclyde describing his group’s outstanding contributions to burgeoning field of Iridium Catalysed Hydrogen Isotope Exchange.

Other topics will include organic synthesis with carbon-14, NMR isotopic hyperpolarisation, new synthetic chemistry relevant to isotopic labelling and new imaging modalities. A detailed programme will follow as soon as it is available.

Contributions to a short series of 5 minute flash presentations covering isotope techniques and methods are actively sought — please contact if you would like to give a presentation. As in past years, there will also be an opportunity to present posters at the meeting — if you would like to present a poster please contact .

This year the meeting will again be held at The Møller Centre in Cambridge, just one mile from the M11 motorway and a short taxi ride from Cambridge railway station with its frequent express services to London, Liverpool Street and Stansted Airport. The meeting will be timed to allow most UK based delegates to travel to and from the symposium on the same day.

We hope you will be able to attend and help make this meeting of the IIS UK Group as successful as in previous years. The closing date for registration is 29th October 2016. Please download and return the registration form with your remittance to confirm your place.
